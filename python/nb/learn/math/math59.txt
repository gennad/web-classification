
Words in {} should be interpreted as greek letters:

Q: I M A {pi}{rho}Maniac.  R U  1,2?
                              o         <- read as "U-not"
A: Y ?
    o

("I am a pyromaniac.  Are you not one, too?"  "Why not?")

F U \{can\} \{read\} Ths U \{Mst\} \{use\} TeX
("If you can read this, you must use TeX")
