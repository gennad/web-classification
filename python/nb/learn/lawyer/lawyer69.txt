A man was sent to Hell for his sins. As he was being taken to his place of
eternal torment, he passed a room where a lawyer was having an intimate
conversation with a beautiful young woman. "What a ripoff," the man
muttered. "I have to roast for all eternity, and that lawyer gets to spend
it with a beautiful woman." Jabbing the man with his pitchfork, the
escorting demon snarled, "Who are you to question that woman's punishment?"
